In this chapter, we explore an erosion model known as the Revised Universal Soil Loss Equation (RUSLE). This model predicts long-term, average annual soil erosion caused by water across various sectors, including agriculture, conservation, mining, construction, and forestry. The project’s goal is to develop soil factors specific to West Kenya.

Source: USDA-Agricultural Research Service, 2024
